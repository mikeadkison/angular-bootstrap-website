Team Fortress 2

Since November of 2015, I have played TF2 competitively on various teams. Right now, I play on two teams concurrently. One team, which I lead, plays a 6v6 (6 players on each team) version of the game, while my other team plays a 9v9 format. I consider TF2 to be the best game in the world, and it seems unlikely to me that a better game will be released even in the next couple decades. If you are interested in playing competitive TF2, check out playcomp.tf for info on how to get started.
